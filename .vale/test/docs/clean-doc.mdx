## How to configure Feature Flags

You can navigate to the settings page to configure your preferences.

1. Click **Save** to save your changes.
2. Select the **Events** option from the dropdown.
3. Navigate to the **Settings** tab.

Check out the [Feature Flags documentation](https://posthog.com/docs/feature-flags) for more details.

- Feature Flags -- A way to toggle features on and off
- Session Replay -- Watch how users interact with your product

PostHog helps you understand how people interact with your app, website, and Product Analytics dashboards.
