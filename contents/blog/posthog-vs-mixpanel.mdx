## How is PostHog different?

### 1. We're an all-in-one platform

PostHog puts all your data in one place and combines it with every tool you need to build a successful product. This means:

- [Product analytics](/product-analytics) for analyzing behavior, funnels, activation, and retention
- [Web analytics](/web-analytics) for tracking your marketing website and content
- [Session replay](/session-replay) for observing how people use your product and diagnosing problems
- [Feature flags](/feature-flags) for testing in production and shipping new features
- [Experiments](/experiments) for verifying improvements to your product and website
- [Error tracking](/docs/error-tracking) for monitoring exceptions and problems in your code 
- [Surveys](/surveys) for capturing user feedback, tracking NPS, and booking interviews
- [A built-in data warehouse](/docs/data-warehouse) for analyzing your business and event data together

In other words, it's everything you need from one app with a single log-in and contract. A _genuine_ single source of truth for your product and customer data.

### 2. It's built for developers

This means you get support from the engineers who _actually build the product_, [extensively documented APIs](/docs/api), and a [SQL query builder](/docs/product-analytics/sql), so you can analyze data how you want. We're open source, so you can inspect [our source code](https://github.com/PostHog) and vote on what we should build via [our public roadmap](/roadmap).

### 3. Transparent and cheap pricing (forever)

We default to charging as little as possible while still making a profit â€“ we also have a generous free tier on all our products. We can do this because we're efficient. We don't splurge on outbound sales, or D-list comedians to host an annual convention you'll never attend. Want to know how much we'll charge? See our [pricing calculator](/pricing).

### Data integrations

PostHog and Mixpanel diverge here:

1. PostHog has a built-in data warehouse, so you can import and query data directly from our other sources like Stripe and Zendesk, or from an existing warehouse. 

2. Mixpanel relies on data warehouse connectors to combine customer and product data. This means you don't import data directly from third-party tools. You need to get them into a third-party warehouse and then import that data into Mixpanel for analysis.

We make this super easy by offering a [managed reverse proxy](/docs/advanced/proxy/managed-reverse-proxy) â€“ a no code solution that takes minutes to setup. We also have [reverse proxy setup guides](/docs/advanced/proxy) for AWS Cloudfront, Caddy, Cloudflare, Netlify, Vercel, and more in our docs.
