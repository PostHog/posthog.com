## What is the PostHog MCP?

The MCP is a server your AI coding agent talks to. You ask a question in English. It runs the query against your PostHog data. The answer shows up in your editor. No SQL, no dashboards, no UI. Just a question, and an answer.

Things you can ask include:

- _"How many unique users signed up in the last 7 days, broken down by day?"_
- _"Create an A/B test for our pricing page that measures conversion to checkout."_
- _"What are the top 5 errors in my project this week?"_

## What does the MCP actually do?

There are 50+ tools spanning product analytics, error tracking, session replay, feature flags, experiments, dashboards, and HogQL. Your agent picks the right one and calls it. You stay in your editor.
