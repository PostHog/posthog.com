    <div>
        <h5>PMF Question</h5>
        <p>"How would you feel if you could no longer use us?" with options: "Not disappointed," "Somewhat disappointed," and "Very disappointed."</p>
    </div>
    <div>
        <h5>A key measure of product-market fit</h5>
        <p>The PMF survey has been used by many startups to measure how close they are to product-market fit. 40% of users saying "very disappointed" is a strong sign.</p>
    </div>
</Section>

<section class="my-12 px-5">

    <h3 class="text-2xl lg:text-3xl m-0 text-center mb-6 sm:mb-12">Getting started with PMF surveys</h3>
    <div class="flex justify-center">
      <ul class="m-0 p-0 list-none inline-grid sm:grid-cols-2 justify-evenly relative after:absolute after:w-full after:left-0 after:top-1/2 after:-translateY-1/2 before:absolute before:h-full before:top-0 before:left-1/2 after:-translateX-1/2 sm:after:block after:hidden sm:before:block before:hidden">
        <li class="relative md:max-w-md py-4 md:py-8 pl-4 pr-2 md:px-12">
          <h5 class="text-xl font-extrabold m-0 pb-1 pr-4">How do you calculate your product-market fit score?</h5>
          <p class="m-0 text-[15px]">Divide the number of users who responded "very disappointed" by the total responses.</p>
        </li>
        <li class="relative md:max-w-md py-4 md:py-8 pl-4 pr-2 md:px-12">
          <h5 class="text-xl font-extrabold m-0 pb-1 pr-4">What's a good PMF score?</h5>
          <p class="m-0 text-[15px]">40% of users saying "very disappointed" is a strong sign of product-market fit.</p>
        </li>
        <li class="relative md:max-w-md py-4 md:py-8 pl-4 pr-2 md:px-12">
          <h5 class="text-xl font-extrabold m-0 pb-1 pr-4">Why use the PMF survey?</h5>
          <p class="m-0 text-[15px]">The PMF survey can give you an idea of your product-market fit over time. Shows if you are heading in the right (or wrong) direction.</p>
        </li>
        <li class="relative md:max-w-md py-4 md:py-8 pl-4 pr-2 md:px-12">
          <h5 class="text-xl font-extrabold m-0 pb-1 pr-4">When to run PMF surveys</h5>
          <p class="m-0 text-[15px]">Use the PMF survey with existing users and run it monthly/quarterly. Use feedback to improve progress toward product-market fit.</p>
        </li>
