## What the Billing team does
 
- Anything billing
  - Billing infrastructure
  - Pricing & self-serve revenue (in collaboration with each product team)
  - Revenue reporting
  - Usage reports
  - Platform plans (Teams / Enterprise)
- Authentication
    - Login and sign up
    - Invites
    - SAML and SSO
    - 2FA
- Platform features
    - Organization settings
    - Access control and RBAC
    - Managed reverse proxy (the feature part, not infrastructure)
    - Authenticated domains
    - Quota limiting (with ingestion team)
    - Organizations / Projects / Environments
