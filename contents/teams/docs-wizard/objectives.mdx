## Missions for 2026

<details>
<summary>We’ve established the AI Wizard as the fastest way to activate PostHog</summary>

This means automatic integration for multiple products across multiple frameworks. This also means support for additional “skills” like migrations and troubleshooting. Ultimately, the Wizard closes the gap between curiosity and real usage. It delivers working PostHog solutions within a user’s codebase in minutes.

</details>

<details>
<summary>We’ve created platform docs that give us a competitive edge</summary>

This means positioning PostHog as a leading developer platform by showing how the entire product suite works together in practice. We’re creating deep, platform-level content like learning paths, cross-product guides, and interactive material for hands-on learning. Success means developers clearly understand PostHog’s strengths and advantages as a dev platform and ecosystem just by reading our docs.

</details>

<details>
<summary>We’ve built systems that make product docs mostly self-serve</summary>

This means each product engineering team writes and maintains their own product docs at a strong baseline level of quality by using the templates, tools, and processes we’ve put in place. We partner with teams to review, improve, or overhaul their docs when needed. Success means keeping current product docs updated and launching several new products with docs, without slowing down.

</details>
