- Enable the creation of **modern, fully isolated products** with CI tests completing in approximately **5 minutes**

- Clarify and standardize **type usage**, including clear boundaries and expectations for product isolation
- Evolve **DevEx engineering best practices**, particularly around product setup, isolation, and maintainability and brief all engineers regularly
- Continuously improve **CI/CD reliability and quality**
  - Identify, track, and reduce flaky tests
  - Implement alerting for red master to ensure fast feedback and recovery
  - Define and track a small set of meaningful CI metrics
- Strengthen and speed up **type checking** across the codebase (e.g. using `ty`) to improve correctness and developer confidence
- Allocate dedicated time for **exploration and experimentation** to inform future technical direction
