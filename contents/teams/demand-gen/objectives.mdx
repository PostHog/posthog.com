## Q1 2026 goals

### Bring in more leads and grow the PostHog inbound surface.

- A full remarketing funnel that allows us to cross-sell our products and introduce our product to people who know us.
- Generate at least 30 qualified inbound opportunities for the sales team. 
- Generate at least 20 qualified outbound opportunities for the sales team. 
- Proper analytics that let us know what ads are performing and why.
- Increased ad variety and copy - new ads going live once per week with the help of propagandist and design contractor resources.
- Through experimentation, determine four pillars of outbound sales: ICP (description and how to source), Persona (description and how to identify), Messaging (content and form), and Channel.
- Identify 3+ campaigns that can reliably generate opportunities. Another BDR can join the team and be successful following the Playbook.
- Design the v0 outbound playbook.
