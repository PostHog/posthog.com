**Paid ads (Brian)**

- Proper analytics that let us know what ads are performing and why
- A full remarketing funnel that allows us to cross-sell our products and introduce our products to people who already know us
- Increased ad variety and copy - new ads going live once per week by the end of the quarter

**Outbound (Lorena)**
- Generate at least one qualified sales opp, as defined in SFDC
- Create the v1 outbound playbook such that another BDR could join and be successful, including:
  - Outbound ICP (definition, where to find them)
  - Outbound personas (definition, where to find them)
  - Messaging (content, form, channels)
- We've run at least 3+ warmbound campaigns that can reliably generate opportunities and are potentially automatable
