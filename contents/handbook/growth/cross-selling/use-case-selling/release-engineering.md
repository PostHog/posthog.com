## What is the job to be done?

"Help me ship faster without breaking things, control who sees what, and validate that changes actually work."

- Safely roll out features to specific users or groups before a full release
- Instantly kill a bad deploy without a rollback
- Measure the impact of a release on key metrics with A/B tests tied to rollouts
- Reproduce user-reported bugs from the user's actual perspective
- Detect quality regressions in AI features after prompt or model changes

## What PostHog products are relevant?

- **[Feature Flags](/docs/feature-flags) (core)** — Controlled rollouts, kill switches, targeted releases
- **[Experiments](/docs/experiments)** — A/B testing tied to releases, measuring impact with statistical rigor
- **[Session Replay](/docs/session-replay)** — Reproduce bugs from user perspective during rollout
- **AI Evals** — Detect quality regressions after prompt or model changes (for AI products)
