## What is the job to be done?

"Help me know when things break, understand why, and fix them fast."

- Catch exceptions and regressions before users report them
- See the user's actual experience when an error occurred
- Understand the business impact of incidents (not just the technical impact)
- Centralize log collection and search alongside error data
- Triage incidents faster with natural language queries

## What PostHog products are relevant?

- **[Error Tracking](/docs/error-tracking) (core)** — Track exceptions, get alerts, resolve issues
- **[Session Replay](/docs/session-replay)** — User impact of errors, visual reproduction
- **[Product Analytics](/docs/product-analytics)** — Error correlation with user behavior and business impact
- **[PostHog AI](/docs/posthog-ai/allow-access)** — Natural language incident triage
- **Logging** *beta* — Centralized log collection and search
