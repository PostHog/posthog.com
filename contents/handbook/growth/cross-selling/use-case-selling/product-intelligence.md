## What is the job to be done?

"Help me understand what users do, why they do it, and what to build next."

- Understand how users navigate your product and where they get stuck
- Identify which features drive retention and which are ignored
- Get qualitative context for quantitative patterns (the "why" behind the numbers)
- Validate product hypotheses with experiments before committing engineering resources
- Collect direct user feedback at key moments in the product experience
- Track business outcomes (revenue, expansion) tied to product usage
- Act on insights by guiding users through onboarding and feature adoption

## What PostHog products are relevant?

- **[Product Analytics](/docs/product-analytics) (core)** — Funnels, retention, cohorts, lifecycle analysis, trends
- **[Session Replay](/docs/session-replay)** — Qualitative layer: watch what users actually do
- **[Surveys](/docs/surveys/creating-surveys)** — Direct feedback loop at key moments
- **[Revenue Analytics](/docs/revenue-analytics/start-here)** — Business outcome tracking
- **[Experiments](/docs/experiments)** — Validate hypotheses with statistical rigor
- **[Workflows](/docs/workflows/start-here)** — Onboarding sequences, activation nudges, lifecycle engagement
- **[Product Tours](/docs/product-tours/start-here)** *alpha* — In-app guided onboarding, feature adoption prompts
- **[PostHog AI](/docs/posthog-ai/allow-access)** — Natural language querying, insight discovery
