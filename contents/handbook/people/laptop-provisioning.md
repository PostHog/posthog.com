# laptop provisioning

PostHog provides laptops and related equipment to team members when needed for their role.

This page explains how laptop provisioning works and how company devices are managed once they exist.

## ordering a laptop

Laptops are ordered through People Ops.

If you need a laptop or replacement device, message the `#team-people-and-ops` Slack channel. People Ops will coordinate ordering and shipping.

Laptops are typically purchased directly by the company rather than expensed individually.

## when your laptop arrives

When your laptop arrives:

1. Set up the device and sign in with your accounts.
2. Install the tools required for your role.
3. Keep the original packaging if possible. It may be useful if the device needs to be returned later.

## replacements

If your laptop stops working or no longer meets the needs of your role, contact People Ops in `#team-people-and-ops`.

People Ops will help determine whether the device should be repaired, replaced, or reassigned.

## leaving PostHog

Laptops and company equipment are expected to be returned when someone leaves the company.

Before your last day:

1. Sign out of your Apple ID or other personal accounts.
2. Remove personal data from the device.
3. Coordinate return shipping with People Ops.

Return instructions may vary depending on your location.

## device lifecycle

Company devices are treated as assets and tracked internally.

A device typically moves through the following stages:

| status | meaning |
|------|------|
| provisioned | device purchased and added to the asset register |
| assigned | device shipped to a team member |
| pending reassignment | returned device available for the next hire |
| returned | device received but not yet reassigned |
| retired | device removed from the fleet |

Tracking devices this way makes it easier to reuse returned laptops and keep visibility of company equipment.

For team members hired through Deel or another employer of record provider, devices are still treated as PostHog company assets and follow the same lifecycle.

## internal operations

People Ops maintains an internal asset register containing company devices and their lifecycle status.

This helps track:

- who currently has each device
- where devices are located
- which devices are available for reassignment
