HogQL is our Direct SQL layer. You can consider it a wrapper around ClickHouse SQL, with a few niceties added.

## HogQL Expressions

![Events table](../images/features/hogql/trends-breakdown.png)

To see your live events table, click 'Events' on the left sidebar. Here you will see a live feed of Events as they are happening.

### Supported ClickHouse functions

Currently the following functions have been enabled. This list is ever expanding.
In case of discrepancies, the version [directly on GitHub](https://github.com/posthog/posthog/blob/master/posthog/hogql/constants.py) has the final say.

#### Arithmetic
- `abs`
- `max2`
- `min2`

#### Type conversions

All properties are currently accessed as strings. To compare numeric properties, you will need to wrap them with a function like `toInt`. For example: `toInt(properties.$screen_width)`

- `toInt`
- `toFloat`
- `toDecimal`
- `toDate`
- `toDateTime`
- `toIntervalSecond`
- `toIntervalMinute`
- `toIntervalHour`
- `toIntervalDay`
- `toIntervalWeek`
- `toIntervalMonth`
