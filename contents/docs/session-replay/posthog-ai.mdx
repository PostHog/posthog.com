PostHog AI can summarize your session recordings, identify behavior patterns, and help you find specific recordings using natural language — so you don't have to watch every replay manually.

> **Not set up yet?** See [Getting started with PostHog AI](/docs/posthog-ai/start-here) for access and setup instructions.

## What you can do

- **Summarize recordings**: Ask PostHog AI to summarize filtered session recordings and categorize them by behavior patterns.
- **Search with natural language**: Find specific recordings by describing what you're looking for (e.g., rage clicks, navigation usage, errors).
- **Analyze experiment replays**: Summarize session replays across experiment variants to understand *why* users behaved differently.

## Summarizing recordings

In the [PostHog AI chat](/docs/posthog-ai/chat), ask PostHog AI to summarize your session recordings. It picks up on any filters you've applied and generates a categorized summary report.

You can ask follow-up questions and request specific recordings to review.

## Searching for specific recordings

Click the <span className="inline-block"><IconSparkles className="h-4 w-4" /> </span> icon next to the session recording filters to search for recordings using natural language.

For example, you can ask PostHog AI to find all recordings where a user rage clicked or got stuck on a particular page.

## Summarizing experiment replays

You can summarize session replays directly from the [Experiments](/docs/experiments) interface:

1. Go to an experiment that has started and has results.
2. Click the **Summarize session replays** button.
3. PostHog AI analyzes user behavior patterns across your experiment variants.

This helps you understand behavioral differences between variants — not just *what* happened, but *why*.

## Example prompts

```
Summarize my session recordings from this week
Find session replays where users got stuck
Show me recordings where users rage clicked
Find recordings of users who used the navigation menu
Summarize session replays from my experiment
```
