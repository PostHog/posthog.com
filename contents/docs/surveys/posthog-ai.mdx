[PostHog AI](/docs/posthog-ai), PostHog's AI-powered assistant, can help you create surveys quickly using natural language. Instead of manually configuring questions, targeting, and display conditions, you can describe what you need and PostHog AI builds the survey for you.

<CalloutBox icon="IconInfo" title="PostHog AI is in beta" type="info">

PostHog AI is currently in beta, so you may encounter occasional issues. We appreciate any [feedback](https://us.posthog.com/home#panel=support) you can share to help us improve.

</CalloutBox>

## What PostHog AI can do for surveys

PostHog AI understands different question types and can:

- **Generate complete surveys** based on your research goals
- **Suggest appropriate question types** (freeform text, rating scales, multiple choice, etc.)
- **Help set up display conditions and targeting**
- **Recommend survey templates** for common use cases

## How to use it

1. Click **PostHog AI** in the top right corner of the PostHog app.
2. Describe what kind of survey you want to create.
3. PostHog AI generates the survey, which you can then review, edit, and launch.

## Example prompts

Try asking PostHog AI things like:

- "Create an NPS survey for my mobile app users"
- "Build a product satisfaction survey with rating questions"
- "Help me create a survey to understand why users churn"
- "Generate a post-purchase feedback survey"
- "Create a PMF survey for new users"
- "Set up a feature feedback survey that only shows to paying users"
