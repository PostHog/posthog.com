You can copy surveys between PostHog [projects](/docs/settings/projects). This is useful when testing a survey in staging before releasing to production, or when deploying the same NPS survey across multiple products.

## How to copy a survey to another project

To copy a survey:

1. Navigate to the survey you want to copy.
2. Click the **...** menu at the top of the survey page.
3. Select **Copy to another project**.
4. Choose the target project from the list.
5. Click **Copy**.

When you copy a survey, PostHog also copies any dependent resources:

- Linked insights (if the survey has one)
- Actions used in display conditions
- Cohorts referenced in display conditions (dynamic cohorts are fully copied; static cohorts are copied but will be empty since the persons may not exist in the target project)

> **Note:** Feature flag targeting conditions are not copied. If your survey uses a linked feature flag, the `linkedFlagVariant` condition will be removed since the flag doesn't exist in the destination project. You'll need to set up a new feature flag in the target project if you want flag-based targeting.
