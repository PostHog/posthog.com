## What you'll accomplish

By the end of this guide, you'll have PostHog installed and capturing data from your app. This typically takes **5–15 minutes** depending on your setup.

<CalloutBox icon="IconInfo" title="New to PostHog?" type="fyi">

PostHog is an all-in-one platform for product analytics, session replay, feature flags, A/B testing, and surveys. Start with the basics below, then explore individual products as you need them.

**Not sure where to start?**

- **Just exploring?** – Use the [snippet installation](/docs/getting-started/install?tab=snippet) to try PostHog quickly
- **Building a web app?** – Follow the chapters below in order
- **Looking for a specific feature?** – Jump directly to [Product Analytics](/docs/product-analytics), [Session Replay](/docs/session-replay), [Feature Flags](/docs/feature-flags), or [Experiments](/docs/experiments)
