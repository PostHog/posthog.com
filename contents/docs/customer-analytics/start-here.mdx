The [customer analytics dashboard](https://us.posthog.com/customer_analytics) works immediately with `$pageview` events. To extract its full potential, configure it with your events in [customer analytics configurations](https://app.posthog.com/project/2/customer_analytics/configuration).

Tips for nailing the setup:

- Use PostHog AI to help you find the events
- Create actions to group multiple events into a "single one"
- Be as specific as possible in event definitions. Sloppy definitions = sloppy metrics.
