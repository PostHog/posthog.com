Opt-out lists track which recipients have unsubscribed from your marketing messages. You can manage opt-outs globally (all marketing messages) or per category (specific message types).

## Add a recipient to the opt-out list

When a customer requests removal through a support ticket or verbal request, you can manually add them to your opt-out list.

1. Go to **Workflows > Opt-outs**
2. To opt someone out of all marketing messages, scroll to the **Marketing opt-out list** section and click **Add opt-out**
3. To opt someone out of a specific category, expand that category and click **Add opt-out**
4. Enter the recipient identifier (typically their email address)
5. Click **Add opt-out** to confirm

The recipient is added immediately and won't receive messages from workflows using the selected category (or all marketing messages if added to the global list).

## Import opt-outs from Customer.io

If you're migrating from Customer.io, see [Import opt-out lists from Customer.io](/docs/workflows/import-customerio-optouts) to bring over your existing unsubscribe data and set up bidirectional sync.
