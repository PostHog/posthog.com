<Steps>

<Step title="Create your endpoint" badge="required">

Once you have your insight or SQL query, create an endpoint from it. This will give you a stable API endpoint you can call to retrieve the data.

<Tab.Group tabs={['Insight', 'SQL']}>
<Tab.List>
<Tab>From insight</Tab>
<Tab>From SQL</Tab>
</Tab.List>
<Tab.Panels>
<Tab.Panel>

1. Open your insight
2. Click on the info & actions button (the three dots button in the top right)
3. Click `Create endpoint`
<ProductScreenshot
  imageLight="https://res.cloudinary.com/dmukukwp6/image/upload/q_auto,f_auto/endpoint_from_insight_b9567d7ef7.png"
  imageDark="https://res.cloudinary.com/dmukukwp6/image/upload/q_auto,f_auto/endpoint_from_insight_b9567d7ef7.png"
  alt="Creating an endpoint from an insight"
  classes="rounded"
/>

4. Give your endpoint a descriptive name and optionally a description
  - **Recommended**: `daily_active_users`, `account_users_activity`, `signup_funnel`
  - **Avoid**: `endpoint1`, `test`, `query`
5. Create the endpoint

</Tab.Panel>
<Tab.Panel>

1. In the SQL editor, click on the `Endpoint` tab in the output pane:

  imageLight="https://res.cloudinary.com/dmukukwp6/image/upload/w_1600,c_limit,q_auto,f_auto/pasted_image_2026_02_06_T12_00_54_246_Z_a8d31a1299.png"

2. Give your endpoint a descriptive name and optionally a description
  - **Recommended**: `daily_active_users`, `account_users_activity`, `signup_funnel`
  - **Avoid**: `endpoint1`, `test`, `query`
3. Create the endpoint

</Tab.Panel>
</Tab.Panels>
</Tab.Group>

</Step>

<Step title="Verify endpoint data" badge="recommended">

Before integrating your endpoint into your application, use the `Playground` tab to verify it returns the expected data.

1. Open your endpoint from the [Endpoints page](https://posthog.com/endpoints)
2. Click the `Playground` tab
<ProductScreenshot
  imageLight="https://res.cloudinary.com/dmukukwp6/image/upload/w_1600,c_limit,q_auto,f_auto/pasted_image_2026_02_06_T12_31_26_778_Z_c5108ec55b.png"
  imageDark="https://res.cloudinary.com/dmukukwp6/image/upload/w_1600,c_limit,q_auto,f_auto/pasted_image_2026_02_06_T12_30_54_702_Z_2d3d04c01b.png"
  alt="Endpoint playground tab"
  classes="rounded"
/>

3. If your endpoint has [variables](/docs/endpoints/variables), enter test values
4. Click **Execute endpoint** to retrieve the data
5. Review the results to understand the response structure

</Step>
