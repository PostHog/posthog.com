PostHog AI powers all AI features within the [PostHog app](https://app.posthog.com). It is an AI agent that sits on top of the PostHog platform. As a result, there are multiple ways of working with PostHog AI.

Here's an overview of the different UIs for interacting with PostHog AI.

## In-app chat

The primary dedicated interface is an in-app <a href="https://app.posthog.com/#panel=max">AI chat</a> for conventional prompting and conversation-based flows.

When logged in, the PostHog AI chat is always available in the right-hand sidebar of the PostHog app. You can open it at any time by clicking the PostHog AI icon.

## Hands-free voice mode

Hands-free mode lets you talk to PostHog AI using your voice instead of typing. It's great for mobile when your hands are busy – at the gym, walking, or commuting.

To enter hands-free mode:
- Press `h then f` on your keyboard, or
- Click the microphone button in the chat input

When active, the chat input transforms into a voice interface with a centered microphone button and status indicator. The mode cycles through these states:

| State | What's happening |
|-------|------------------|
| **Listening** | PostHog AI is listening for your voice. Start speaking your question. |
| **Thinking** | Your question was received. PostHog AI is working on an answer. |
| **Speaking** | PostHog AI is speaking the response aloud. |

Interrupt PostHog AI while it's speaking by starting to talk – this automatically returns to listening mode so you can ask a follow-up or redirect the conversation.

In hands-free mode, PostHog AI formats responses to sound natural when spoken aloud. Numbers and currencies are spelled out – "one hundred dollars from five thousand users" instead of "$100 from 5,000 users" – and percentages appear as words like "twelve point five percent." Responses also skip markdown formatting, code blocks, and emoji. This makes answers easier to follow when you're listening rather than reading.

To exit hands-free mode, tap the microphone button or press `h then f` again.

## Platform UI

PostHog AI shows up throughout the PostHog platform as inline or ambient UI elements, typically labelled with the <span className="inline-block"><IconSparkles className="h-4 w-4" /> </span> icon, and embedded within other features.

These entrypoints or shortcuts enable you to trigger PostHog AI directly from the PostHog UI.
