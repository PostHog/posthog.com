title: Evaluations
---

Evaluations help you measure and improve the quality of your LLM outputs. Use automated checks to score generations at scale, manual reviews to dig into individual traces, datasets to test prompt changes against known inputs, and taggers to organize and filter generations for analysis.

## In this section

- [Evaluations](/docs/ai-evals/evaluations) – Run LLM-as-a-judge or code-based (Hog) checks against your generations.
- [Trace reviews](/docs/ai-evals/trace-reviews) – Manually review individual traces with structured scorers and queues.
- [Datasets](/docs/ai-evals/datasets) – Curate input/output pairs you can replay against prompt changes.
- [Taggers](/docs/ai-evals/taggers) – Automatically classify and label generations for downstream analysis.

## Related products

- [AI Observability](/docs/ai-observability) – Capture traces, generations, and spans from your LLM application.
- [Prompt Management](/docs/prompt-management) – Manage prompts and skills you can iterate on without code deploys.

## Installation

Evaluations run against data captured by AI Observability. If you haven't set up capture yet, follow the [AI Observability installation guide](/docs/ai-observability/installation).
