<CalloutBox icon="IconWarning" title="Revenue analytics dashboard is being retired" type="warning">

The Revenue analytics dashboard will be removed on or after **June 30th, 2026**.

We're shifting revenue tracking to expose revenue properties on persons and groups across insights, SQL, and profiles. This lets you build the view your team needs – whether that's ecommerce, SaaS, recurring, or one-off revenue – using [Product Analytics insights](/docs/product-analytics/insights), [SQL queries](/docs/data-warehouse/query), or [PostHog AI](/docs/posthog-ai).

Your revenue data and configurations aren't going anywhere. You can keep capturing revenue events and querying revenue data through the data warehouse and managed views.

</CalloutBox>
