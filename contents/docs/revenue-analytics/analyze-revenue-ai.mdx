title: Analyze revenue with PostHog AI
---

[PostHog AI](/docs/posthog-ai) tracks revenue trends, builds dashboards, and analyzes your business performance using natural language. Ask about MRR, churn, revenue by segment, or plan comparisons – and PostHog AI queries your [revenue data](/docs/revenue-analytics) to find answers.

## How it works

PostHog AI can work with your revenue data to:

- **Track revenue over time** – MRR, ARR, total revenue, and growth rates
- **Break down by dimension** – revenue by product, plan, region, or customer segment
- **Compare periods** – this quarter vs last quarter, month-over-month growth
- **Identify trends** – growth drivers, churn patterns, and revenue concentration risks

Revenue data comes from your connected sources (like [Stripe](/docs/data-warehouse/sources/stripe)) or custom revenue events you've captured.

## Revenue metrics PostHog AI can calculate

| Metric | What it measures | Example prompt |
|--------|-----------------|----------------|
| **MRR** | Monthly recurring revenue | "Show MRR for the past 12 months" |
| **ARR** | Annual recurring revenue | "What's our current ARR?" |
| **ARPU** | Average revenue per user | "Compare ARPU across plans" |
| **Churn** | Revenue lost from cancellations | "What's our revenue churn rate this quarter?" |
| **Expansion** | Revenue growth from existing customers | "How much expansion revenue did we add last month?" |
| **LTV** | Lifetime value by segment | "What's the average LTV for enterprise customers?" |

## Tips for better results

- **Name your data source** – "from Stripe" or "from our `purchase_completed` events" helps PostHog AI query the right tables
- **Specify the metric** – "MRR" vs "total revenue" vs "ARPU" tells PostHog AI exactly what to calculate
- **Include comparison dimensions** – "by plan" or "by country" adds breakdowns to the analysis
- **Set time ranges** – "this quarter" or "year over year" scopes the query to the right period
