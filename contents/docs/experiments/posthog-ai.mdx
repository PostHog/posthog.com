PostHog AI helps you go beyond the numbers in your experiment results. Instead of just seeing *what* changed between variants, you can use PostHog AI to understand *why* users responded differently — by automatically analyzing session replays across your experiment variants.

## Summarize session replays across variants

When reviewing experiment results, you can have PostHog AI summarize session recordings from each variant to identify behavioral differences.

To use this:

1. Go to an experiment that has started and has results.
2. Click on any variant in the delta chart to open the detailed results panel.
3. Click the **Summarize session replays** button.
4. PostHog AI opens and automatically analyzes user behavior patterns from session replays across your experiment variants.

PostHog AI categorizes recordings by variant and identifies behavioral differences, helping you understand not just what happened in your experiment, but why users responded differently to each variant.

## What you'll learn

PostHog AI reviews session recordings across your variants and provides:

- **Behavior pattern categorization** — groups recordings by common user behaviors within each variant.
- **Cross-variant comparison** — highlights differences in how users interact with each variant.
- **Key findings summary** — surfaces the most important behavioral insights so you don't have to watch every recording yourself.

## Example prompts

After PostHog AI opens with your experiment context, you can ask follow-up questions like:

```
Summarize session replays from my experiment
What behavioral differences do you see between variants?
Why might users be converting better in variant B?
Which variant has more rage clicks or signs of frustration?
```
