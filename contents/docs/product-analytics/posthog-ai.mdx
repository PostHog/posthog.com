title: PostHog AI for product analytics
---

[PostHog AI](/docs/posthog-ai/overview) is an AI-powered product analyst that lives inside PostHog. For product analytics, it can help you build insights, write queries, create dashboards, and answer product questions — all using natural language.

## What PostHog AI can do

When working with product analytics, PostHog AI can:

- **Build insights and visualizations** from natural language descriptions — trends, funnels, retention charts, paths, and more.
- **Write and debug HogQL queries** for advanced analysis, including queries across data warehouse tables.
- **Create and customize dashboards** with multiple insights tailored to your needs.
- **Analyze your data** by running queries, interpreting results, and summarizing what it finds.
- **Answer product questions** by exploring your events, person properties, cohorts, and actions.

## How to access PostHog AI

Click the PostHog AI icon in the bottom corner of the app to open the chat panel. It's available on every page, so you can start a conversation from any insight, dashboard, or query view.

You can also add context to your conversation — like a specific insight, dashboard, or event — so PostHog AI can reference the exact data you're looking at.

## Example prompts

Try these prompts to get started:

```
- Build a funnel showing signup to first purchase.
- What are my top 10 events by volume this week?
- Create a retention chart for users who completed onboarding.
- Write a SQL query to find users with the highest session count.
- Which user properties correlate with conversion?
- Build a dashboard tracking our key activation metrics.
```

## Getting started

PostHog AI requires a one-time approval from an organization admin. See [Getting started with PostHog AI](/docs/posthog-ai/start-here) for setup instructions.
