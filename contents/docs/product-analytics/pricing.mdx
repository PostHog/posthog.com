Product Analytics comes with a generous free tier and transparent, usage-based pricing. Our large free tier means more than 90% of companies *use PostHog for free*.

No credit card is required to get started. You can also set billing limits to avoid any surprise charges.

Product Analytics is billed by the number of events captured. The price per event changes based on your usage. You can estimate your costs using our pricing calculator below or by visiting our [pricing page](/pricing) for a more detailed breakdown.
