The self-hostable Loom alternative. Record and share screen recordings asynchronously without relying on external services. Features unlimited recordings of tabs, desktops, and applications, public link sharing, automatic deletion or unlisting after specified timeframes, and support for uploading existing videos. Built with Next.js, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS.
