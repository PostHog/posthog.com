<summary>How can I refer another startup?</summary>

<p>To refer someone, simply tell them to apply as normal and to specify your company name in the application form. We'll review and issue an additional $5,000 of PostHog credit to both you, and them.</p>

<p>There are currently no limits on how many startups can be referred into the program successfully.  </p>

</details>

<summary>I signed up before this deal launched, can I still get it?</summary>
